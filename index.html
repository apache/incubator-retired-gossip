Apache Gossip™ (incubating)

Apache Gossip™ protocol is a method for a group of nodes to discover and check the liveliness of a cluster.

Apache Gossip

Apache Gossip™ is a system to form peer-to-peer networks using the gossip protocol.

Apache Gossip™ (incubating) is an effort undergoing Incubation at The Apache Software Foundation (ASF), sponsored by the Incubator. Incubation is required of all newly accepted projects until a further review indicates that the infrastructure, communications, and decision making process have stabilized in a manner consistent with other successful ASF projects. While incubation status is not necessarily a reflection of the completeness or stability of the code, it does indicate that the project has yet to be fully endorsed by the ASF.
